Lasagna

Description

Lasagne is a layered Italian dish made with sheets of pasta, rich meat or vegetable sauce, creamy béchamel, and melted cheese. It's baked until golden and bubbly, offering a hearty, comforting meal.

Ingredients

For the meat sauce

  • Ground beef
  • Onion, finely chopped
  • Garlic, minced
  • Tomato sauce or crushed tomatoes
  • Tomato paste
  • Diced tomatoes
  • Olive oil
  • Red wine (optional)
  • Italian seasoning (Basil, oregano, thyme)
  • Salt and pepper
  • Sugar (optional, to taste)

For the béchamel sauce

  • Butter
  • All-purpose flour
  • Milk
  • Nutmeg
  • Salt and pepper

For assembly

  • Lasagne sheets (fresh or dried)
  • Ricotta cheese (optional)
  • Parmesan cheese, grated
  • Mozzarella cheese, shredded

Optional add-ins

  • Fresh basil leaves
  • Spinach or other vegetables
  • Red pepper flakes (for heat)

Steps to make the lasagna

Prepare the meat sauce

  1. Heat olive oil, in a large pan.
  2. Cook onions until translucent and then add garlic for another minute.
  3. Add the meat and break it up with a spoon. Cook until brown.
  4. Stir in tomato sauce, tomato paste and diced tomatoes.
  5. Add italian seasoning and red wine, if you're using it.
  6. Reduce to a simmer and let it cook for 20-30 minutes.

Prepare the béchamel sauce

  1. Add 4 tablespoons of butter in a saucepan over medium heat.
  2. Add 4 tablespoons of flour and keep whisking continously to form a paste. Cook for 1-2 minutes don't let it brown.
  3. Gradually add 4 cups of milk while whisking to prevent lumps.
  4. Once the sauce thickens, season with salt, pepper and a pinch of nutmeg. Remove from heat.

Assemble the lasagna

  1. Preheat the oven to 190°C
  2. Layer ingredients:
    1. Spread a thin layer of meat sauce on the bottom.
    2. Place a layer of lasagne sheets on top.
    3. Cover the sheets with more meat sauce
    4. Add a layer of bechamel sauce
    5. Sprink with ricotta -- If using
    6. Add parmesan cheese
    7. Repeat the process
    8. Finish with a layer of bechamel on top and sprinkle with shredded mozzarella and the remaining parmesan cheese.
  3. Add optional ingredients

Bake the lasagna

  1. Cover the baking dish with aluminium foil and bake in the preheated oven for 25-30minutes.
  2. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 15-20 minutes.
  3. Let the lasagna rest for 10-15 minutes before slicing and serving.

Serve

  1. Cut the lasagna into square and serve.
